RAYNA TOURS BOOKING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

My Bookings Page
================
Page URL: https://www.raynatours.com/bookings?filters={"filters":{"status":"all","state":"past"}}

URL Parameters:
- Replace "state" with: "upcoming" or "past"  
- Replace "status" with: "all", "confirmed", "cancelled", "completed", or "booking_in_process"

Booking Tabs & Filters
=====================
1. Upcoming: Shows future bookings
2. Past: Shows previous bookings

Status Filter Options:
- All: Shows all bookings regardless of status
- Confirmed: Shows confirmed bookings only
- Cancelled: Shows cancelled bookings only  
- Completed: Shows completed bookings only
- Booking In Process: Shows bookings currently being processed

Search Functionality:
- Search bar available to find bookings by tour name

Booking Card Information
=======================
Each booking card displays:
- Tour image
- Tour name
- Booking date
- Location/destination
- Transfer type (if applicable)
- Guest count/number of people
- Booking status badge (confirmed/cancelled/completed/etc.)

Available Actions on Booking Cards
=================================

Get Help Action:
- Button available on every booking card
- Opens support/contact options for that specific booking
- Use this for any booking-related issues or questions

Download Ticket Action:
- Available for confirmed bookings
- Downloads the booking ticket/voucher
- Contains all booking details and QR codes

Rate Experience (Past Bookings):
- "How was your experience?" option
- Allows customers to leave star ratings
- Only available for completed past bookings

Driver Details:
- Viewable directly on booking card
- Shows for confirmed bookings that include transfers
- Contains driver name, contact, and vehicle information

Downloadable Documents
=====================
Available downloads from booking cards:
1. Voucher - Booking confirmation document
2. Ticket - Entry ticket with QR codes
3. Invoice - Payment receipt and booking details  
4. Cancellation Confirmation - If booking was cancelled

Cancellation & Refund Process
============================
Cancellation Policy:
- Cancelled bookings are automatically refunded to the Rayna Wallet
- Wallet URL: https://www.raynatours.com/wallet
- Refunds are processed instantly to wallet balance

Using Wallet Balance:
- Wallet balance can be used to book any new tour or activity
- No restrictions on which tours can be booked with wallet credit
- Balance never expires

Pickup Requests & Changes
========================
For any pickup request changes (time, date, or location):
- Go to "My Bookings" page
- Find the relevant booking
- Use "Get Help" button for assistance

Date Change Policy & Process
===========================
Important: Rayna Tours does not support direct date changes on existing bookings.

Recommended Fast Process:
1. Cancel your current booking (refund goes to wallet instantly)
2. Rebook the same tour with your preferred new date
3. Use your wallet balance to pay for the new booking
4. This is the fastest way to change your booking date

Alternative Support Option:
- If you prefer manual assistance with date changes
- Click "Get Help" on your booking card
- This connects you directly to our support team
- Support team can provide personalized assistance

Booking Status Explanations
===========================
- Confirmed: Booking is confirmed and guaranteed
- Cancelled: Booking has been cancelled, refund processed to wallet
- Completed: Tour/activity has been completed
- Booking In Process: Payment received, booking being processed
- All: Shows bookings in any status

Customer Support Access
======================
Multiple ways to get help:
1. "Get Help" button on individual booking cards
2. General customer support via website
3. Phone support for urgent matters
4. Email support for detailed inquiries

Best Practices for Customers
============================
- Always check booking status before travel date
- Download tickets/vouchers before your tour
- Keep driver details handy for transfer bookings  
- Use wallet balance for quick rebookings
- Contact support early if you need assistance
